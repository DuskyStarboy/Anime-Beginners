What is Anime?

Anime is Japanese animated content — often hand-drawn or digitally animated. Anime is famous for its unique art styles, deep stories, and emotional soundtracks.

It covers many genres:

  • ✨ Shonen (少年) → Action & adventure for teen boys (ex: Naruto, One Piece)

  • ✨ Shojo (少女) → Romance & drama for teen girls (ex: Sailor Moon, Your Lie in April)

  • ✨ Seinen (青年) → Mature action & drama for adults (ex: Berserk, Tokyo Ghoul)

  • ✨ Josei (女性) → Romance & life stories for adult women (ex: Nana, Paradise Kiss)

  • ✨ Isekai (異世界) → “Other world” — character transported to fantasy world (ex: Re:Zero, Sword Art Online)

  • ✨ Mecha (メカ) → Giant robots & machines (ex: Gundam, Evangelion)

  • ✨ Slice of Life → Everyday life stories, relaxing (ex: Clannad, Barakamon)

  • ✨ Iyashikei (癒し系) → Healing anime, calm & peaceful (ex: Natsume’s Book of Friends)

  • ✨ Magical Girl (魔法少女) → Girls with magical powers (ex: Cardcaptor Sakura, Madoka Magica)

  • ✨ Sports → Competitive sports anime (ex: Haikyuu!!, Kuroko no Basket)

  • ✨ Psychological → Deep thinking, mental games (ex: Death Note, Monster)

  • ✨ Supernatural → Spirits, magic, monsters (ex: Bleach, Jujutsu Kaisen)

  • ✨ Harem / Reverse Harem → One character surrounded by many love interests (ex: Ouran High School Host Club)

  • ✨ Yaoi / BL (Boys Love) → Romance between boys

  • ✨ Yuri / GL (Girls Love) → Romance between girls
